Privacy Policy

The important bits about how we look after your information.

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Who we are

Happy Magpies is a jewellery and accessories brand operated in the United Kingdom.

For questions about this privacy policy or how we use your personal information, please email hello@happymagpies.co.uk .

Information we collect

When you join the Happy Magpies mailing list, we may collect:

  • Your first name
  • Your email address
  • The date and time you subscribed
  • Information about your interaction with our emails

Our website provider may also process limited technical information, such as your IP address, browser type and device information, in order to deliver and protect the website.

How we use your information

We use your information to send you Happy Magpies news, including:

  • Launch announcements
  • New jewellery and accessory collections
  • Market dates and events
  • Special offers and other brand updates

Our lawful basis

We rely on your consent to send marketing emails. You provide this consent when you enter your details and choose to join our mailing list.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in one of our emails or by contacting us.

Mailchimp

We use Mailchimp to manage our mailing list and send emails. Information submitted through our sign-up form is therefore processed by Mailchimp on our behalf.

Mailchimp and its service providers may process information outside the United Kingdom. Mailchimp states that it uses appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

Who we share information with

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate our website and mailing list, including Mailchimp and our website hosting provider. These providers may only use the information to provide their services to us.

How long we keep your information

We keep your mailing-list information while you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we may retain limited information to ensure that we respect your request and do not send you further marketing emails.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may have the right to:

  • Ask for a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Ask us to restrict how we use your information
  • Object to certain uses of your information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@happymagpies.co.uk .

Complaints

Please contact us first if you have any concerns about how we use your information.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office, the UK regulator for data protection.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy when our website, services or legal obligations change. The latest version will always be published on this page.
